Star Wars Title Crawls & credits

When I think of credits rolling, I also think of the opening title crawl from the Star Wars franchise. So this webpage includes just that! The opening title crawl along with the credits for each film in the series.

Episode I The Phantom Menace

				Turmoil has engulfed the
				Galactic Republic. The taxation
				of trade routes to outlying star
				systems is in dispute.

				Hoping to resolve the matter
				with a blockade of deadly
				battleships, the greedy Trade
				Federation has stopped all
				shipping to the small planet
				of Naboo.

				While the Congress of the
				Republic endlessly debates
				this alarming chain of events,
				the Supreme Chancellor has
				secretly dispatched two Jedi
				Knights, the guardians of
				peace and justice in the
				galaxy, to settle the conflict....
